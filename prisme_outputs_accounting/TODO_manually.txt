To set the Prisme headers and footers for the reports :

1. Go to settings and activate developper mode
2. Under General Setting > Business Document > Format, select your paper format (usually A4) and open it with the external link button.
3. Change bottom margin to 15mm.
4. Save.

5. Under General Setting > Business Document > Document Layout, clic on "Edit Layout".
6. Paste the following XML inside the root <t t-name="..."> :

	<div t-attf-class="header o_company_#{company.id}_layout">
	    <div class="row">
	        <div class="text-center w-100">
	            <img t-if="company.logo" t-att-src="image_data_uri(company.logo)" style="max-height: 100px;" alt="Logo"/>
	        </div>
	    </div>
	</div>
	
	<div t-attf-class="article o_report_layout_standard o_company_#{company.id}_layout pt-5" t-att-data-oe-model="o and o._name" t-att-data-oe-id="o and o.id" t-att-data-oe-lang="o and o.env.context.get('lang')">
	    <t t-call="web.address_layout"/>
	    <t t-raw="0"/>
	</div>
	
	<div t-attf-class="footer o_standard_footer o_company_#{company.id}_layout">
	    <div class="text-center" style="border-top: 2px solid #f4946c;">
	        <div name="financial_infos">
	            <span t-field="company.report_footer"/>
	        </div>
	    </div>
	</div>
    
7. Save and it's done.
